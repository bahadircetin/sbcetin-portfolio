In 2015, I came to Ankara for my education at Bilkent University after high school.

Bilkent is a tough school and gives a real importance to English level of students. That is why, preparation school took 2 years before my department.

My department is a CTIS, which inside of applied sciences faculty. I spend really good time in there. I owe lots of things to this department, I learned a lot from my prominent professors and friends also. One of the wonderful thing that I thank to almost every course had lab sessions so we tried and applied everything we learned in theoric class lectures in these lab sessions. In addition, lab sessions and lectures have different grading systems. We took grades from labs and lectures separately for a single course. This system was challenging of course, because we had a lot of things to work on and also we had a lot of assignments separately for lecture and labs. I had a lot of deadlines for one semester along with midterms and finals. Another good thing was term projects. Almost every course has a term project which aims to bring your learnings together inside a real project. By doing this, you are getting encouragement to build more projects because you are seeing the capability to build a real software project in yourself.
